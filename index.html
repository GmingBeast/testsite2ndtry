About Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a natural healing system that originated in India more than 5000 years ago.

It focuses on maintaining balance between the body, mind and spirit.

According to Ayurveda the body is governed by three energies called Vata, Pitta and Kapha.

Ayurvedic treatments use natural herbs, oils and detox therapies.

These treatments help improve digestion, immunity and mental health.

Ayurveda focuses on curing the root cause of diseases rather than just symptoms.

Healthy lifestyle, proper diet and yoga are essential parts of Ayurvedic living.
